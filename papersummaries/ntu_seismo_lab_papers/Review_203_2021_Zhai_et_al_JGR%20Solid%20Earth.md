catalogs, especially during or right after extreme weather events. Here we apply the template matching method
to build a more complete earthquake catalog in Taiwan spanning seven months before and 12 months after
2009 typhoon Morakot, which brought the highest rainfall in southern Taiwan in the past 60 years and triggered
