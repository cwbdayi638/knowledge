üìç About NACB Station

NACB is a broadband seismic station that continuously monitors ground motion. The station records three components of seismic waves: vertical (Z), north-south (N), and east-west (E).

üìä Data Information

Waveforms show 10 minutes of continuous seismic data. Data is fetched from IRIS FDSN web services and updated every 30 minutes. This page auto-refreshes to show the latest available data.

üîç How to Read

Each trace represents ground motion in one direction. Look for sudden changes or oscillations that may indicate seismic events. Background noise reflects microseismic activity and environmental factors.
